What This Library
Needs to Grow

The Convergence Project was built by one person — with AI tools, years of research, and a deep need to make the truth visible and accessible. The infrastructure is here. The decks are expanding. Now it grows with the community.

Here is what is needed and how you can help:

💻 Tech Volunteers

Web developers, engineers, and IT professionals — help maintain, improve, and expand the site infrastructure as the library grows.

🔬 Research Contributors

Data analysts, academics, researchers — submit sourced data, flag errors, and help verify and expand the decks. All submissions reviewed before publishing.

🏫 Educators & Organizers

Teachers, community leaders, advocates — collaborate on how this material gets used in classrooms, community spaces, and policy conversations.

📣 Amplifiers

Share the library. Post a deck. Put it in a group chat. Show it at a meeting. The most powerful thing you can do is put this data in front of someone who needs it.

To get involved, submit a correction, or propose a collaboration — reach out directly.

Data Integrity

See an Error?
Say Something.

Every stat in this library links to a primary source. But sources get updated, studies get revised, and mistakes happen. If you find a number that is wrong, a link that is broken, or a claim that needs better sourcing — flag it.

Send the deck name, the stat in question, and your source. Everything gets reviewed before any change is published. Corrections are always credited.
