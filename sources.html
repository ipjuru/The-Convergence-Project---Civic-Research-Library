How to Use This Source Bucket

Every source listed here links directly to the original document — a government database, peer-reviewed study, or nonpartisan institution. To fact-check a specific stat from a deck, use the Jump to Deck nav above to find that deck's sources, then click ↗ Fact Check next to the relevant entry.

Link shows a 404? Government and institutional sites occasionally reorganize their pages. Try searching the source title + institution name to find the updated URL. If a stat feels alarming — that's the point of checking it. Scrutiny is welcome here.

Contested areas are flagged at the bottom of each deck section — these are places where the science or data is still actively debated. That context matters. This library does not present contested findings as settled fact.
